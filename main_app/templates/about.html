About Mai SIS

Mai SIS is built off of my previous experience with Student Information Systems (SIS), specifically with Aspen and PowerSchool.

Before you start to explore the links in the nav, you should read the directions to confirm how to navigate the site.

Directions

Now that you are logged in, you will need to go to the staff link and click 'add staff' so that you can connect your account to a staff account.

After creating a staff account, you will be able to create your own courses and add students to them.

Students will only be able to enroll/unenroll from classes you are the instructor for.

All courses are listed, but not all are linked. That's because you are only able to access the courses you are the instructor for.
